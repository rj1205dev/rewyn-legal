Delete your Rewyn account and data

Rewyn (by Simple Craft) · Last updated: 19 June 2026

You can permanently delete your Rewyn account and all of your data at any time. There are two ways to do this.

Option 1 — In the app (instant)

  1. Open Rewyn and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

This permanently and immediately removes your account and all associated data.

Option 2 — By email (if you no longer have the app)

Email rj1205dev@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Rewyn account, with the subject line "Delete my account". We will verify and delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes all of your data, including:

  • Your profile (email and pay-cycle settings)
  • All expenses you have logged
  • All bills and recurring costs
  • All budget categories and limits
  • All savings goals
  • Your pay-cycle history

No personal data is retained after deletion. Subscription/billing records held by Google Play are managed by Google under its own policies.
