Rewyn by Simple Craft

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Rewyn mobile application ("Rewyn", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your information. Rewyn is a budgeting app operated by Simple Craft, based in Sydney, Australia. By creating an account or using Rewyn, you agree to this Policy.

1. Information we collect

We only collect what is needed to run the app. We do not link to your bank, and we do not use advertising or third-party tracking/analytics SDKs.

  • Account information: your email address and a password (the password is stored only as a secure hash by our authentication provider, so we never see it in plain text).
  • Financial information you enter: the figures you type into the app. This includes your income, pay-cycle settings, expenses (amount, category, optional note, date), bills/recurring costs, and savings goals. This data is entered manually by you; we never access your bank accounts or transactions.
  • Subscription information: if you purchase Rewyn Pro, your purchase and subscription status is processed by Google Play Billing and managed via RevenueCat. We receive your subscription/entitlement status. We never receive or store your payment card details.
  • Technical information: standard information necessary to deliver the app (such as your authentication session). We do not collect your precise location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers for advertising.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide the budgeting features (calculate your disposable income, track spending against your pay cycle, show progress on goals and bills).
  • To create and secure your account and keep you signed in across sessions.
  • To process and manage your Rewyn Pro subscription.
  • To send essential account emails, such as password-reset messages.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. Service providers

We share data only with the processors that make the app function:

  • Supabase: database, authentication, and secure storage of your account and financial data.
  • RevenueCat: subscription management.
  • Google Play Billing: payment processing for subscriptions.
  • Resend: delivery of transactional emails (e.g. password reset).

These providers process your data on our behalf and are bound to protect it.

4. Storage and security

Your data is stored in our Supabase database and protected by row-level security so that each account can only access its own data. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). We retain your data for as long as your account exists.

5. Deleting your data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app: Settings → Delete account. This removes your profile, expenses, bills, categories, savings goals, and cycle history. You may also request deletion by emailing us at the address below, and we will action it.

6. Your rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal information. Most of this is available directly in the app; for anything else, contact us using the details below.

7. Children

Rewyn is not directed at children and is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version. Material changes will be communicated within the app where appropriate.
